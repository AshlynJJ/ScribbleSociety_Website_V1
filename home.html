FAQ

How did Scribble Society come to be?

The founder of the company, Ashlyn Jordan January, she was part of a group of friends who loved creating art but did not want to be judged. So the founder thought of Scribble Society. Fun Fact! The founder has only thought about this company hypothetically until someone important to her, pushed her to actually give Scribble Society a chance.

FAQ

Why the name 'Scribble Society'?

The founder of the company wanted the company to have a playful and light hearted feel to the company. To give the message of "There is no judgement, only guidance" to all ages, to give a bit of confidence to show the world their Bold, honest and creative Art.
